These are some things to try as you're playing Order in the Court! There are some alternative game styles and things to try to spice up the game.

Order in the Court: Drinking Game Version

This game can easily be made into a drinking game. The Judge can give out drinks as penalties to players who get caught in a lie, do something annoying, or however the judge pleases. Feel free to use penalties even if you don't want to involve alcohol!

Points for Puns

Judges can use an criteria they want to make their ruling. In early versions of this game, we often found puns to be a particularly good way to judge guilt.

Crime & Punishment

In the normal game, the judge just decides "GUILTY" or "NOT GUILTY". The game is more fun if the judge also gives out a punishment, whether within the story or real life.
