Start Case
You can start the case once The Judge, The Plaintiff, and The Defendant have picked their names.
  • You are in charge of directing the case. How you do this is up to you!
  • After all evidence and witnesses are revealed, you will make a verdict.
  • Optionally, you can use the "Case File" below to keep score and take notes.
  • If there are witnesses in this case, you can decide to call them at any time. Do this by telling the witness player(s) to reveal a witness.
  • If there is a jury in this case, you can allow them to have an input on the verdict at the end of the case.
