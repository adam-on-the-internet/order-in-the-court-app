Create a case

To make a case, pick a case name and decide how much evidence and how many witnesses to have. The more witnesses and evidence, the longer the game will last. You can also pick a standard 3, 4, or 5 person game so you don't have to decide how many witnesses and evidence to use.

Evidence per side must be 1 to 11.

Witnesses must be 0 to 11.
